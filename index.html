Cool Places

Places I have seen that are cool

Jerusalem

The Holy City

  • A city which contains sacred sites for all three Abrahamic religions
  • My favorite things to do:
    1. Visit the Pools of Bethesda and sing in the Church of St. Anne
    2. Usher in Shabbat at the Western Wall
    3. Buy 6 shekel Monster energy drinks in the Armenian Quarter
  • Apart from this city's deep history, it is special to me because of the people I met there.

A view from the Old City of the BYU Jerusalem Center and the Mount of Olives

Mexico City

La Gran Ciudad

  • I served the second half of my mission here from June 2021 - June 2022
  • Once an ancient Aztec city, Mexico City is now one of the largest cities in the world
  • These are some of my favorite places to eat in CDMX:
    1. Super Tacos El Chami
    2. Taquerias La Onda
    3. Madre Cafe
    4. La Boruega Viuda
  • Visitors of Mexico City must make it a priority to make it to a Lucha Libre match. Matches happen often at La Arena Mexico.

Llubljana

The City of Dragons

  • This quaint city in Slovenia gets its nickname from the many artworks of Dragons throughout the city.
  • There is a lot of great restaraunts to eat at, and the city is fun to explore. It is like a mini Paris.
  • Lake Bled, a beautiful lake, is only a short ride away. Its a great place to explore and relax.
